Welcome to Waverley Cafe

Delicious coffee, cakes, breakfast and lunch

Discover our nature-based venue, nestled within the beautiful Brisbane botanic gardens.

Residing within a converted greenhouse, we serve seasonally evolving and locally sourced dishes and fresh, aromatic coffee made with love by our passionate baristas.

Whether you're looking for a quick coffee or bite or a relaxing dine in experience, you will have the best experience with Waverley Cafe.
